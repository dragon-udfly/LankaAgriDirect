# Software Feature Specification: Lanka Agri-Direct (v1.0)

## 1. Features for Buyers (Consumers - Anonymous)

The buyer experience focuses on frictionless discovery of fresh produce across Sri Lanka without the need for account creation.

* **Nationwide Visual Product Feed:** A real-time display of available harvests (Fruits, Vegetables, Grains, Herbs) across all provinces of Sri Lanka.
* **Coordinate-Based Search & Filters:** Advanced tools to browse items by specific category, district, and proximity to the buyer's current GPS location.
* **National Map Integration:** A location-based view showing the physical distribution of verified producer stalls and collection points across the country.
* **Direct Contact Access:** One-tap access to view a producer's business phone number and collection address to facilitate direct physical transactions.
* **Anonymous Browsing:** A "no-login" experience that allows buyers to search and find producer details immediately upon opening the app.
* **Personalized Local Bookmarks:** A "Star" feature allowing buyers to save favorite producers directly to their phone's local storage. This provides a "Favorites" list for frequent purchases without requiring a buyer login.

## 2. Features for Producers (Farmers)

Producers are provided with a "Digital Storefront" that prioritizes identity trust and simplified stock management.

* **Verified Onboarding Workflow:** A mandatory registration process requiring the upload of National Identity Card (NIC) photos and bank details for Admin approval.
* **One-Touch "Sold Out" Toggle:** A simplified manual inventory control allowing producers to instantly hide a listing from public search once the stock is physically sold.
* **Business Profile Display:** A dedicated profile page showcasing the producer's operating hours, Grama Niladhari (GN) division, and business type (e.g., small-scale or home gardener).

## 3. System & Admin Features

Core backend and administrative tools ensure platform integrity, data security, and performance.

* **Account Verification Console:** A specialized portal for System Admins to review producer identity documents and manually verify accounts to unlock listing privileges.
* **Lead Analytics Tracking:** Backend logic to monitor buyer engagement by tracking clicks on "Call Farmer" and "View Address" buttons.
* **Immutable Audit Logs:** An append-only system log that records all administrative actions and producer status changes for security monitoring.
* **Data Privacy & Encryption:** Advanced protection of sensitive information, including the encryption of producer bank account numbers and the secure storage of NIC photos.
* **English-Only Interface:** A standardized interface designed for high performance and clarity, exclusively in the English language.
